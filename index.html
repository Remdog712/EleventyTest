Simple Lemonade Recipe

Refresh yourself with this easy-to-make lemonade!

  • 1 cup of freshly squeezed lemon juice (4-6 lemons)
  • 1/2 cup of sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 4 cups of cold water
  • Ice cubes
  • Fresh mint leaves (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a pitcher, combine the lemon juice and sugar. Stir until the sugar dissolves.
  2. Add the cold water and mix well.
  3. Fill glasses with ice cubes and pour in the lemonade.
  4. Garnish with mint leaves if desired. Serve and enjoy!
